ABOUT CONVOKE

Convoke is the annual techfest of Cluster Innovation Centre, University Of Delhi. We are one of the biggest techfest in the north campus and aim to give unparraleled amount of exposure to our participants and tech enthusiasts.

More than 600 participants last year from more than 60 colleges from around the country. From late night coding sessions to ravagin robotics events, we have it all. See you there!!
