ROBOLINEA

Date: 19th October

Time: 10AM Onwards

Prizes Worth: 15K

Team Size: 1-4 members.

Description

You are on the run. A burgulary, a theft. Dont look back, just run. Run until the track ends or your breath runs out. But i only say one thing Dont look back.

Your bot has to make a run for it. Be the fastest of all to make it to the top. If your breath runs out go back home. Its simple. Survival of the fastest. See you at the finishing line. Sayonara!

Rules

Maximum Team size is 4.

Team members may belong from different educational institutions, and they need to bring ID proof of their education on the day of event.

The team is not allowed to view the track before competition, violation of this rule will lead to disqualification.

The team must start in the direction of the volunteer present at the event.

Any wireless communication on the bot must be switched off. Violation will lead to disqualification.

The maximum voltage at a given point of the robot must be less than equal to 24 V.
